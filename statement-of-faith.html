statement of faith

We exist to glorify God by demonstrating His love to the children, youth and the widows. we provide comprehensive medical and educational services and a loving, Christ-centered home and placement services for vulnerable and orphan children.

We believe!

TFM Core values

  • We are Christ-centered and prayer focused.
  • We are committed to keeping families together.
  • We value the lives of all children, regardless of their limitations or challenges.
  • We value adoption and make it a primary placement option for most of our children.
  • We value our ugandan and expatriate staff, their gifts and distinct cultural perspectives.

TFM statement of faith

  • We believe the Bible to be the only inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. -(2 Timothy 3:16)
  • We believe there is one God, the Creator of all beings, Who is eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. -(Matthew 3:16-17; John 1:18; John 15:26)
  • We believe in the deity of Christ, in His sinless life, and in His vicarious and atoning death on the cross for our salvation.
  • We believe that all who place their faith in Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord are born again children of God.
  • We believe in the great Commission; “Go ye into all the world and preach the Gospel to every creature.” -(Mark 16:15)
  • We believe in the Spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ. -(Ephesians 4:3-4)
Financial Accountability

Trust Future Ministries receives a four star rating for financial accountability. We treasure and thank you all who stand with us to serve the indigent people in our community.
